Our Story

Dira Tarot was founded in 2015 by spiritual guide Maria Dira, who discovered her intuitive gifts at a young age. What began as personal readings for friends has blossomed into a full-service spiritual center offering tarot, energy healing, and sacred tools for transformation.

We believe everyone has the power to connect with their intuition and higher self. Our mission is to provide the guidance and tools to help you on your journey.
